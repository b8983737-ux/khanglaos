Core features

Everything to control food cost — in one place

Most Lao restaurants run on memory and notebooks. KHANGLAOS turns that into numbers you can act on, without changing how your kitchen works.

📦

Scan to receive & use

Staff scan a barcode with the phone camera to receive deliveries or take stock out. No long forms, no typing — so they actually use it.

🍲

Profit per dish

Build recipes once. KHANGLAOS calculates the true cost and profit of every dish from live ingredient prices, and flags the ones losing money.

🔎

Loss finder

See what recipes say you used versus what actually left stock. The gap is theft, over-portioning, or unrecorded waste — in money.

⏰

Expiry & FEFO

The system always uses the oldest stock first and warns you before things expire — one tap to record waste with the cost attached.

🛒

Auto purchase orders

When stock runs low, KHANGLAOS drafts the order with quantities and last prices filled in. You just review and send. No typing.

👥

Manager vs staff

Owners and managers see all the money. Staff see only their work — never your margins, costs, or profit. Enforced, not just hidden.

Questions

Frequently asked

Do I need to buy any hardware?+
No. KHANGLAOS runs in any web browser on the phone, tablet or computer you already have. The phone camera is your barcode scanner — nothing to install or buy.
Does it work in Lao language?+
Yes. The whole system is fully translated into Lao, Thai, English, Chinese and Korean. Staff pick their language on the login screen; everything follows.
Will my staff actually use it?+
It's designed around that exact problem. Staff scan and tap — they almost never type. Expiry, location and prices are filled in automatically. If a step can be removed for staff, it has been.
Can staff see my profit and costs?+
No. Management (owner/manager) sees all the money. Staff see only stock and their tasks — never margins, food cost or profit. This is enforced at the database, not just hidden in the screen.
How is this different from a spreadsheet?+
A spreadsheet can't scan a barcode, can't warn you before food expires, can't tell you which dish is unprofitable in real time, and can't catch the gap between what you should have used and what actually left stock. KHANGLAOS does all of that automatically.
How do I start?+
Open KHANGLAOS, create your restaurant, add a few items, and start scanning. You can be tracking real stock the same day — no long setup project.
