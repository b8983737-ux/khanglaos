Questions

Frequently asked

Do I need to buy any hardware?+
No. KHANGLAOS runs in any web browser on the phone, tablet, or computer you already have. The phone camera is your barcode scanner — nothing to install or buy.
Does it work in Lao language?+
Yes. The whole system is fully translated into Lao, Thai, English, Chinese and Korean. Staff pick their language on the login screen; everything follows.
Will my staff actually use it?+
It's designed around that exact problem. Staff scan and tap — they almost never type. Expiry, location, and prices are filled in automatically. Most steps that exist in spreadsheets are removed entirely for staff.
Can staff see my profit and costs?+
No. Management (owner / purchasing manager) sees all the money. Staff see only stock and their tasks — never margins, food cost, or profit. This is enforced at the database, not just hidden in the screen.
How is this different from a spreadsheet?+
A spreadsheet can't scan a barcode, can't warn you before food expires, can't tell you which dish is unprofitable in real time, and can't catch the gap between what you should have used and what actually left stock. KHANGLAOS does all of that automatically.
How long does setup take?+
Most restaurants are tracking real stock by the end of day one. Setup is: create account, add your top 20 items, train staff to scan (5 minutes per person). You can grow the catalog as you go.
What about data privacy and security?+
All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. We use Supabase + Cloudflare infrastructure in Singapore — closest region to Laos. Your supplier prices, recipes, and margins are visible only to roles you authorize.
